The Problem

Healthcare professionals are drowning in noise. Patient safety is at risk.

Missed Patient Needs

Urgent symptoms and referral requests get buried under hundreds of admin emails, causing dangerous delays in care.

Clinical Time Waste

Doctors and nurses spend hours manually filtering billing questions and scheduling requests instead of treating patients.

Triage Bottlenecks

Without automated scoring, every email looks crucial. Critical P1 emergencies sit in the same inbox as routine feedback.

Compliance Risks

Manual handling leads to privacy slips and missed SLA deadlines, increasing legal liability and patient dissatisfaction.

The Solution

Intelligent Inbox Triage designed for Healthcare Organizations.

Smart Categorization

Our AI instantly separates Clinical Triage from Billing, Scheduling, and Admin, so the right team sees the right email.

Priority Routing

P1 Emergencies bypass the queue for immediate action, while P3/P4 routine items get automated or batched responses.

Seamless Integration

Connects securely with Outlook and EHRs, maintaining HIPAA compliance without changing your daily workflow.

Core Features

Engineered for the modern workflow.

7-Domain Classification

Automated Reply

Enterprise Security

Outlook Sync
